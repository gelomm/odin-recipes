Avocado Watermelon Salad

Angelo Maliksi Mendenilla

Description

This is a very healthy, yet delightfully different salad.

A flavorful addition could be small slices of red onion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed Watermelon
  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach, torn
  • 2 large avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
  • ¼ cup walnut oil
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • ½ teaspon sweet paprika

Directions

There are only 2 easy steps on making this healthy salad.

    Step 1

  1. Combine Watermelon, spinach, and avocados in a bowl.

    2. Step 2

  2. Whisk walnut, olive oil, lime juice, and paprika together in a bowl; pour over Watermelon mixture. Toss to coat.
